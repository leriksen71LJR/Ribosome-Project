# Versioning-Deprecation-Strategy.md

**Type:** Process Strategy

**Purpose:** Provide guidance on how to handle versioning and deprecation of documentation, procedures, and contracts over time.

## Recommendations
- When changing behavior or contracts, clearly document the change and any migration path.
- Prefer deprecation over immediate removal when possible.
- Maintain backward compatibility notes where relevant for agents and humans.

## Notes
This strategy supports long-term maintainability and reduces the risk of breaking changes surprising future agents or contributors. It can evolve as the project matures.
