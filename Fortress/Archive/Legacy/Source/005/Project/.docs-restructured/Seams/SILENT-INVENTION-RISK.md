# SILENT-INVENTION-RISK.md

**Purpose:** Document the risk of silent invention (invention that occurs but is not disclosed in the build report).

## Example from Phase 1.2A
During the shootout, one agent implemented per-connection keying for encryption without disclosing it in the Significant Issues or Risks section (though it was present in code).

## Impact
Silent invention reduces the effectiveness of disclosure as a quality control mechanism and can hide important specification gaps from stewards.

## Recommended Mitigation
- Strong encouragement of honest disclosure
- Consideration of an Invention Summary table in combined disclosure formats
- Steward spot-checks of High-risk seams in code when disclosure is silent

## Notes
This seam highlights the importance of disclosure honesty as a quality dimension.
