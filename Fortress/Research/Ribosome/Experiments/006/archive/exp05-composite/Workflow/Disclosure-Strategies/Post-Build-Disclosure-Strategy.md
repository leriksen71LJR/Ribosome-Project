# Post-Build-Disclosure-Strategy.md

**Type:** Disclosure Strategy

**Purpose:** Defines the mandatory post-build disclosure process (Rule 12).

This strategy can be replaced or extended by other disclosure strategies in the future.

## Requirements
- Must be the final step of every build.
- Must produce `REASONING-YYYY-MM-DD-XXX-{Agent}.md` in the project root.
- Must follow the required sections defined in the core disclosure protocol.
- Must be honest and retrospective, based on actual implementation performed.

## Notes
- Pre-build disclosure is deprecated for standard builds.
- This strategy focuses on making documentation performance visible to stewards.

*Alternative disclosure strategies can be developed and composed as needed.*
