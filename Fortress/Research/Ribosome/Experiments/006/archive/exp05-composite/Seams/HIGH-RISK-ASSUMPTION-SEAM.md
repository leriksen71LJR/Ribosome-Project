# HIGH-RISK-ASSUMPTION-SEAM.md

**Purpose:** Provide guidance on how to handle high-risk assumptions during implementation.

## Requirements
- High-risk or architectural assumptions must be explicitly flagged (Rule 11).
- They should be prominent in the build report and disclosure.
- Justification and severity must be provided.

## Notes
High-risk assumptions that are not surfaced can lead to significant divergence between agents and hidden specification debt. This seam type should be actively monitored.
