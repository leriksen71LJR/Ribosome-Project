# DEFERRED-PHRASE-SEAM.md

**Purpose:** Document the risk of "deferred phrases" in specifications.

## Examples
- "archive/delete per storage design"
- "as appropriate"
- "handle according to requirements"

## Problem
These phrases outsource decisions to the agent, leading to convergent invention that is often undocumented.

## Recommended Action
Replace deferred phrases with either:
- Explicit enumerated behavior, or
- A named Decision Procedure link

## Notes
Deferred phrases were identified as a significant source of T2 invention during the Phase 1.2A analysis. This seam type should be actively avoided in future specifications.
