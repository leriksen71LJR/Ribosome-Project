# Long-term-Maintainability.md

**Type:** Analytical Perspective (Strategy)

**Purpose:** Evaluate documentation and structural decisions based on their sustainability over 6–12 months or longer.

## Key Questions
- How sustainable is this documentation structure or content over time?
- Will it create technical or process debt?
- How easy will it be to evolve, prune, or extend later?

## Application Guidance
Use this perspective when making decisions that have long-term structural implications, such as adding new files, folders, or mechanisms.

## Notes
This perspective often favors simpler, more focused structures that are easier to maintain and evolve. It should be balanced with other perspectives such as Effort vs Impact and Archaeology / Future Maintenance.
