# Archaeology-Future-Maintenance.md

**Type:** Analytical Perspective (Strategy)

**Purpose:** Evaluate decisions based on how well they support future understanding of why things were done a certain way.

## Key Questions
- How easy will it be for a future person (or future version of ourselves) to understand why we made these decisions?
- Does this leave good “archaeological” traces?

## Application Guidance
Use this perspective when deciding what historical context, decision rationale, or superseded content should be preserved.

## Notes
This perspective often favors selective preservation of history without bloating active documentation. It should be balanced with Single Responsibility and Long-term Maintainability.
