# Versioning-Deprecation.md

**Type:** Analytical Perspective (Strategy)

**Purpose:** Evaluate documentation and structural decisions based on how well they support versioning and deprecation over time.

## Key Questions
- How easy is it to evolve or deprecate parts of the documentation without causing confusion?
- Are there clear mechanisms for handling old vs. new approaches?

## Application Guidance
Use this perspective when planning changes to documentation structure, decision procedures, or quality mechanisms. Good versioning and deprecation practices reduce long-term confusion and technical debt.
