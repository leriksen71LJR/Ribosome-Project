# Metrics-Success-Criteria.md

**Type:** Analytical Perspective (Strategy)

**Purpose:** Define how success should be measured for documentation and process improvements.

## Key Questions
- How would we measure whether a documentation change or new strategy is working?
- What signals would tell us it is reducing invention or improving clarity versus just creating more documentation?

## Application Guidance
Use this perspective when designing feedback mechanisms, audits, or new quality strategies. Prefer leading indicators and qualitative signals over narrow quantitative targets that could trigger Goodhart effects.

## Notes
This perspective is especially relevant when combined with Invention Disclosure and Documentation Audit strategies.
