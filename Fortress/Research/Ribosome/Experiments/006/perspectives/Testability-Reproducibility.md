# Testability-Reproducibility.md

**Type:** Analytical Perspective (Strategy)

**Purpose:** Evaluate how easily builds, disclosures, and workflows can be reproduced and tested.

## Key Questions
- How deterministic and reproducible is this process or documentation?
- Can another agent (or future version of ourselves) reliably produce similar results from the same inputs?
- What makes certain parts harder to test or reproduce?

## Application Guidance
Use this perspective when reviewing process strategies and disclosure mechanisms. Higher testability and reproducibility generally lead to more reliable feedback loops and fewer hidden inventions.

## Notes
This perspective is particularly relevant when designing combined disclosure formats (Era C) and automated checks.
