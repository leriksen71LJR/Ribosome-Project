# Risk-Perspective.md

**Type:** Analytical Perspective (Strategy)

**Purpose:** Provide a lens for identifying and evaluating risks in documentation and implementation.

## Key Questions
- What could go wrong if we follow this documentation?
- What are the failure modes (process theater, hidden invention, increased complexity, agent confusion)?
- How severe and likely are those risks?

## Application Guidance
Use this perspective when reviewing specifications, especially at component seams and exception cases. Prioritize High-severity risks for immediate documentation improvement.

- Effort vs Impact

- Edge Cases & Failure Modes
